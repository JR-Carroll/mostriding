Three Wheeled Motorcycle - Basic Rider

Course for Trykes or Spyders

Available for trikes or Spyders, this new course from the Motorcycle Safety Foundation is just what you need to get a good feel for riding your trike or spyder. It's a fun way to get started or brush up your skills. You can use your own machine or one of our newly converted GZ250 trikes! Sorry but sidecars are not allowed.

Get Pricing Take a class Buy Certificate

Course size is limited to a maximum of 6 students for two instructors.

Tuition for this course is $430.00.


This is a license waiver course for the residents of New Hampshire, Maine and Massachusetts!

What to Wear

  • Long Pants
  • Over the ankle sturdy footwear
  • Long sleeved shirt or jacket
  • Full-fingered gloves
  • Eye protection
  • DOT approved helmet - we can provide you with a helmet if you need one

You must have all of these in order to participate in the class.
