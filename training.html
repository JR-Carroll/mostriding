Basic Rider Course

The Basic Rider Course is a license waiver course designed for the beginner rider as well as those who have spent many years on two wheels. Successful completion of this course will will grant the student a license waiver that will allow them to get their motorcycle endorsement without any further testing.




View details »

Experienced Rider Course

The Experienced Rider Course is designed for riders who are already licensed and ride frequently. Students may repeat the course periodically to keep skills and perceptions about safety fresh. This course is performed on your own motorcycle to build confidence.





View details »

Returning Rider Course

The Returning Rider Basic Rider Course is a new 8 hour course designed for riders who are returning to riding after an absence. This program combines elements of the Basic Rider Course (BRC) and the Experienced Rider Course (BRC2) including a knowledge test and a skill evaluation.




View details »

Three Wheel Rider Course

Available for trikes or Spyders, this new course from the Motorcycle Safety Foundation is just what you need to get a good feel for riding your trike or spyder. It's a fun way to get started or brush up your skills. Successful completion of this course will grant the student a license waiver that will allow them to get their three wheel endorsement without any further testing.
