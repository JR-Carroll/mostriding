Experienced Rider Course

Sharpen your riding skills while exposing some of those bad habits you may have picked up over the years.

Get Pricing Take a class Buy Certificate

The Experienced Rider Course is designed for riders who are already licensed and ride frequently. Students may repeat the course periodically to keep skills and perceptions about safety fresh. Riders must have their own street-ready motorcycle.

All riders must successfully achieve the objectives of Exercise 1 (Control at Low Speed) to continue. The Rider Coach will make this determination.

Check with your insurance company as many offer a discount for successful completion of a rider safety course.

Tuition for this course is $100.00

What to Wear

  • Long Pants
  • Over the ankle sturdy footwear
  • Long sleeved shirt or jacket
  • Full-fingered gloves
  • Eye protection
  • DOT approved helmet - we can provide you with a helmet if you need one

You must have all of these in order to participate in the class.
