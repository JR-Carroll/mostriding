Gift Certificates

We have gift certificates available for our Basic Rider Course, Experienced Rider Course, and 3-Wheeled Motorcycle Course.

We also have $100 gift certificates that can be used towards any class in the upcoming season.

To order using online payment, fill out the form below then pay using the Buy Now buttons. If you have any questions or to place a phone order, you may contact us at 800.503.6678. Please feel free to leave a message. We are a seasonal business but we make it a point to return phone calls promptly.

Gift certificates are usually mailed out within 24 hours of payment being received.

Anyone receiving a gift certificate will be considered an early registrant. They will be notified via email approximately one week before the schedule is published in order to sign up for their class.
